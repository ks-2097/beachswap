## 📧 Email to the club owner
**Subject:** A simple way to earn more from the courts that sit empty

Hi [Owner name],

Quick idea I'd love your take on.

Every week, a handful of season-pass holders can't use their booked court — they're away, injured, or just can't make it. Right now that court sits empty and nobody benefits.

I put together **BeachSwap**: a simple web app where a pass-holder can put their slot back on the market for a given week. Another player books it, pays the pass-holder directly, and **the club automatically earns a commission on every swap** — a rate you set. Fuller courts, happier members, and a new revenue line with zero extra admin for you.

It's already a working demo (no sign-up needed) — there's even an owner dashboard showing every booking, your commission on each, and who owes what. Have a 2-minute click around: [LINK]

Could I grab 15 minutes this week to show you? I think there's real money currently being left on the sand.

Cheers,
[Your name]
